Phishing

What is Phishing?

A cyberattack where attackers disguise themselves as trusted entities to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information.

Types of Phishing

Includes email phishing, spear phishing, and SMS phishing, all targeting users to gain unauthorized access to data.

How to Prevent Phishing

Always verify the source of emails and messages, avoid clicking on suspicious links, and use anti-phishing software.
